	Vim Class 2022
	3. Verbs
	   Repeat atomic changes with . (dot)

	Why use . (dot)?

	* Reinterprets command in new position

	* Works with inserts:  ablah<esc>.

	* Works with search:  /W<enter>gUen.n.nn.

	* Works with blocks:  ctrl-Vjjer@w.ww.ww.
