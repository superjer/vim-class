	5. Modes
	   Visual mode & external commands (:!)

	Go together like two things that go together really well

        :'<,'>!wc
        :'<,'>!column 
        :'<,'>!sort | uniq -c

        '<,'> indicates that the command is operating over the
	range of the visual selection. You don't need to type it.
