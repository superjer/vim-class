
#	3. Verbs
##	   Repeat atomic changes with . (dot)

	Why use . (dot)?

	* Reinterprets command in new position

	* Works with inserts:  ablah<esc>.

	* Works with search:  /W<cr>gUen.n.nn.

	* Works with blocks:  <c-V>jjer@w.ww.ww.
