	Vim Class 2022
	6. Plugins
	   Make life easier with a plugin manager

	vim-plug by Junegunn
	https://github.com/junegunn/vim-plug
	
	Example .vimrc config:

        call plug#begin()
        Plug 'maxbrunsfeld/vim-yankstack'
        Plug 'airblade/vim-gitgutter'
        Plug 'tpope/vim-surround'
        call plug#end()
